How to Install Our App

Before downloading:

  • Ensure you have enough storage space
  • A stable internet connection is recommended
  • You may need to enable "Install from Unknown Sources" in your device settings

Important Security Note

Your device might show a security warning about installing apps from unknown sources. This is normal for apps not downloaded from the Google Play Store.

Installation Steps:

  1. Click the "Download App" button
  2. Open your Downloads folder
  3. Tap the downloaded .apk file
  4. If prompted, go to Settings and enable "Install from Unknown Sources" for your browser
  5. Return to the download and tap "Install"
  6. Wait for installation to complete
  7. Tap "Open" to launch the app
